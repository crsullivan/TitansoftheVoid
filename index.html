ABOUT TOTV

A distant and unique solar system, home to industrious inventors, ruthless cutthroats, and bewildering creatures. The only thing rarer than it's star, are the strange, small nebulae woven between it's planets.

Our adventurers all begin as citizens of Vitalion, a very small but beautiful planet. There is only one continent, a large volcanic island. The climate is chilly and precipitous, 77F at the equator and an average of 10F at the southernmost and northernmost reaches. The planet is sparsely populated, but there are two governing bodies, Kigania in the East and Wendenta in the West. Vitalion is primarily a mining planet, but it is also known for producing some of the best ship making lumber in the solar system.
